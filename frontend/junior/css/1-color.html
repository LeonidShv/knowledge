Colors

There are few formats of colors:

by name
red, green, blue
by HEX
#fff, #f1f1f1
by RGB
rgb(255, 155, 0)
by HSL
hsl(hue, saturation, lightness)
hue - 0 is red, 120 is green, and 240 is blue
Saturation - 100% is pure color, 50% - with grey
Lightness - 0% is black, 100% - is white
opacity

0.5

a = 0.5
