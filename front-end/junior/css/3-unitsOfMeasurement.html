Units of measure

Absolute Lengths

  1. px * pixels (1px = 1/96th of 1in), main absolute unit
  2. cm centimeters, 1cm (см) = 38px
  3. mm millimeters, 1mm (мм) = 3.8px
  4. in inches (1in = 96px = 2.54cm)
  5. pt points (1pt = 1/72 of 1in), 1pt (типографский пункт) = 4/3 px
  6. pc picas (1pc = 12 pt), 1pc (типографская пика) = 16px

Relative Lengths

  1. em Relative to the font-size of the element
  2. rem Relative to font-size of the root element
  3. vw Relative to 1% of the width of the viewport*
  4. vh Relative to 1% of the height of the viewport*
  5. % Relative to the parent element
  6. vmin Relative to 1% of viewport's* smaller dimension
  7. vmax Relative to 1% of viewport's* larger dimension
  8. ex Relative to the x-height of the current font (rarely used)
  9. ch Relative to the width of the "0" (zero)
